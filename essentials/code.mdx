### How to build an agent

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select to create an agent">
    ![](/Step1.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter a description of what you want your agent to be capable of">
    In natural language describe your agent and what functionality it has. This will be used to create a system prompt for your agent.

    ![](/Step2.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select your agent's functionality">
    Select between 1 - 10 functions. Think of these as the tools or skills your agent will have. All your available plugins will be available.

    ![](/Step3.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Give your agent some details">
    Upload an image, give your agent a name, description and set a sale price in SOL.

    ![](/Step4.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Time to fill out your about section">
    Please upload a markdown file or paste in your text. This will be used for the about section and explain to people what it does.

    ![](/Step5.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review your agent details">
    Look over the details of your agent and pay 500 credits for your agent to be deployed.

    ![](/Step6.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
