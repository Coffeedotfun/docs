## **Credit System**:

$COFFEE is used as credits for transactions and interactions within the Coffee platform.

<Info>
  Credits can be purchased using $COFFEE, ETH, or SOL.
</Info>

### Burn Mechanism

All $COFFEE collected through the app is burned, creating a deflationary mechanism to increase scarcity and value. Additionally, all of the ETH and SOL from buying credits and marketplace fees are using to buyback and burn $COFFEE.

<Info>
  Total Burned: 1,000,000
</Info>
